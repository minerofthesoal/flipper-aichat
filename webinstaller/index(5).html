devboard flasher

Flashes the AI Chat bridge firmware straight from this page onto the Flipper Zero WiFi Devboard's ESP32-S2, over USB. No installer, no drivers, no PlatformIO required.

before you flash
  1. Use Chrome or Edge on desktop — Web Serial isn't available on iOS, and Firefox/Safari don't support it.
  2. Plug the WiFi Devboard into your computer with a data-capable USB cable, on its own (detached from the Flipper is fine, or attached — either works over its own USB port).
  3. Put the ESP32-S2 into bootloader mode first: hold BOOT, tap RESET once while still holding BOOT, then let go of BOOT after a second or two. The devboard has no USB-UART bridge chip, so nothing does this for you automatically — skip it and the page will just sit on "installing…" / "preparing installer" forever with no error.
  4. Click Connect below, then pick the devboard's serial port from the browser's port picker.
  5. The write should start immediately and finish in well under a minute. If it instead hangs on "preparing installer," the board wasn't in bootloader mode — unplug, redo the BOOT/RESET step above, and click Connect & Install again.
  6. Leave the cable in place until the page reports finished — disconnecting mid-write can brick the flash and require a manual recovery.
